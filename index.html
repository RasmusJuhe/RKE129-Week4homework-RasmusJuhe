Omelette Recipe

Ingredient Amount
Eggs 4
Salt 5 ml
Pepper 2 ml
Milk 15 ml

Instructions

  1. Step 1 : Crack the eggs into a bowl
  2. Step 2 : Add salt, pepper and milk
  3. Step 3 : Mix them together
  4. Step 4 : Heat the pan
  5. Step 5 : Pour the mixture to the pan
  6. Step 6: Fry until fluffy and ready
  7. Step 7 : Enjoy!
